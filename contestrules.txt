## SPIF and Sales Contest Request Guidelines

### Timeline Requirements
- All requests must be submitted minimum 3 weeks before quarter start
- No communication about contests allowed before official approval

### Request Process
1. Submit initial request (3+ weeks before quarter)
2. Schedule consultation with Sales Compensation team
3. Complete Intake Form (sent via email)
4. Review proposal with Sales Compensation team
5. Await President of Sales approval
6. Launch documentation preparation with Communications team

### Approval Chain
1. Sales Compensation team creates proposal
2. President of Sales reviews and approves
3. Communications team prepares launch materials

### Important Notes
- All SPIFs and Sales Contests require President of Sales approval
- Early communication before formal approval is strictly prohibited
- Launch documents will be created only after approval

### To begin the process
Please provide:
- Full Name
- Email Address
