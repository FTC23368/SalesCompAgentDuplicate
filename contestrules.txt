Here are a few important points with respect to SPIF and Sales Contests requests:

1. All requests should be initiated at least 3 weeks BEFORE the start of the quarter.

2. Book an appointment with Sales Comp team member to go over your requirements.

3. In order to prepare for this meeting, please fill out the Intake form which will be sent to you via email.

4. The Sales Comp team will send the proposal to the President of Sales for approval. Please note that all SPIFs and Sales Contests 
must be approved by the President of Sales. Any communication about the SPIF or Sales Contest to employees before 
formal approval is strictly prohibited. 

5. If approved, the Sales Compensation team will work with Communications team to create launch documents.

Please confirm that you understand these rules and we will start appointment booking.
